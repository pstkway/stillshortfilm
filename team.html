actors

Alexandra Sakellaropoulou is a renowned Greek actress of film, theatre and television. In 1989 she won the Karolos Koun Leading Actress Award for her performance in Faith, Love and Hope (Horváth.) From 1992 until 2001 she lived and worked in France, collaborating with the Théâtre de l’Instant. Since her return to Greece she has starred in a variety productions, including the hugely successful TV series, The Island (MEGA).




Giannis Georgoudis, Maria Fergadi, Amerissa Mavrikou and Amar Benkhalad are non-actors, residents of the island of Skyros.

writer & director

Mavra Peponi is a Greek filmmaker currently working in Los Angeles. She received a BA in Film and Video from Georgia State University in 2012, and worked for two years on a variety of film and TV sets, including Taken 3 (Luc Besson), Adult Swim and Tyler Perry productions. On May 2017, she received an MFA in Film Directing from California Institute of the Arts (CalArts). In 2017-2018 she was a Post-Graduate Teaching Fellow at CalArts, teaching original courses on movement and dance in film. She is an Onassis Foundation and Hollywood Foreign Press Association scholar.

director of photography

Jesse Mazuch was born in Kerpen 1984 and raised in Cologne, Germany. Since 2008 he works as a focus puller and cinematographer, while also studying at the German Film and Television Academy Berlin, participating in classes by Sophie Maintigneux, Michael Bertl, Fred Kelemen, Andres Veiel and Nina Menkes. Several of the films he shot (HERMAN THE GERMAN, SATURNTAGE and others) have been nominated and awarded at important film festivals, including Berlinale, Max-Ophüls-Preis, Cannes Next Generation Short Tiger and First Steps Awards. Please visit jessemazuch.com to view Jesse’s Reel.
