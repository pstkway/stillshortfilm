Ten days after the death of her beloved, Athina arrives on a Greek island to clear out the summer house where they lived together.
For three days we watch her open windows and cupboards, sort things out and pack boxes.
On the day of her departure and into the night, Athina is still on the porch in front of the empty house, by the edge of the sea.
